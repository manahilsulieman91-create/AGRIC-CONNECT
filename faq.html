Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common queries about our marketplace.

How do I register as a farmer/seller? +

Click on "Sign Up" in the navigation menu. You'll need to complete the KYC process by providing your government ID, farm details, and bank or mobile money account. Approval takes 1-2 business days.

What are the fees for selling on Agric Connect? +

We charge a 5% transaction fee on completed sales. Farmers keep 85-90% of the selling price – much higher than traditional markets. No listing or monthly subscription fees.

How is product quality ensured? +

We have a 3-step verification: 1) Farmer self-declaration with photos, 2) Random physical checks by our field agents, 3) Buyer ratings & feedback system. You can request refunds for any quality issues.

How do I get paid for my produce? +

Payments are released to your verified mobile money (MTN/Airtel) or bank account within 24 hours after the buyer confirms delivery or after 3 days automatically.

Can buyers return products? +

Yes, for damaged or incorrect items. Returns must be requested within 48 hours of delivery. We mediate disputes and ensure fair resolution for both parties.

What areas do you currently serve? +

We currently operate in all major districts of Uganda: Kampala, Jinja, Mbale, Gulu, Mbarara, and Fort Portal. Delivery to neighboring countries will begin in late 2025.

Is there a minimum order amount? +

For individual buyers, no minimum. For bulk/wholesale orders, the minimum is 50kg of grain or equivalent. Check each seller's product page for their specific batch sizes.
